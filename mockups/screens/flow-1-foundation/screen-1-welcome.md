## Flow 1: Foundation

### Screen 1: Welcome

**Prompt:**

Design a Welcome/Onboarding screen for "Printy".

**Layout:**
Centered single column. Clean and bold.

**Visual Centerpiece:**
A large, pixelated or dithered icon of a thermal printer or a "receipt" rolling out. The logo "PRINTY" in JetBrains Mono ExtraBold at the top.

**Typography:**

- Title: "PHYSICAL ARTIFACTS" in JetBrains Mono, Uppercase, 24px.
- Subtitle: "For the Digital Age." in Inter, 16px, Graphite color.
- Body: "Connect your thermal printer and start making."

**Key Elements:**

- **Logo**: Top center.
- **Hero Image**: Dithered aesthetic illustration.
- **Primary Action**: "Start Printing" (Big button, 2px border, hard shadow).
- **Secondary Action**: "Sign In" (Text link).

**Interaction Notes:**

- Button has a "mechanical" press effect (moves down 4px).
- Background has a subtle grain texture.

**States:**

- **Loading**: Logo pulses.

**Vibe:**
Industrial cool. Like unboxing a new gadget.

**Animation Note:**
Elements snap in one by one. Title -> Subtitle -> Image -> Button.
