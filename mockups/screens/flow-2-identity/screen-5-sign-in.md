## Flow 2: Identity & Pro

### Screen 5: Sign In

**Prompt:**

Design the Sign In / Authentication screen.

**Layout:**

- **Centered Card**: Floating in the middle of the screen.
- **Top**: "PRINTY_ID" header.
- **Body**: Auth providers list.
- **Footer**: Terms link.

**Visual Centerpiece:**
The "Access Card" aesthetic. The login form looks like a physical ID badge or keycard.

**Key Elements:**

- **Header**: "AUTHENTICATE" in monospace.
- **Providers**:
  - "Continue with GitHub" (Black button, white text, GitHub icon).
  - "Continue with Google" (White button, black border).
  - "Continue with Email" (Outline button).
- **Divider**: "— OR —" monospace.
- **Email Input**: If selected, rectangular input with "Enter email..." placeholder.

**Typography:**

- Labels: "ACCESS_LEVEL: GUEST" (top right of card).

**Interaction Notes:**

- Buttons have a solid hover state (invert colors).
- Success state: The card "slides" down as if inserted into a reader.

**Vibe:**
Secure entry. "Access Granted".

**Animation Note:**
Card flips in 3D on load.
