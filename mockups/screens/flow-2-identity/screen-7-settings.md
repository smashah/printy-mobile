## Flow 2: Identity & Pro

### Screen 7: Settings

**Prompt:**

Design the Settings screen.

**Layout:**

- **Standard List**: Grouped items with headers.
- **Top**: User Profile snippet.

**Visual Centerpiece:**
Clean, organized typography. Minimal distractions.

**Key Elements:**

- **Profile Section**: Avatar (dithered), Name, "PRO" badge (if active).
- **Groups**:
  - **General**: "Printer Settings" (Default density, Paper size).
  - **Account**: "Subscription", "Restore Purchases", "Sign Out".
  - **Support**: "Help Center", "Privacy Policy", "Terms".
  - **App**: "Version 1.0.0 (Build 420)".

**Typography:**

- Headers: "SYSTEM", "ACCOUNT", "INFO" in small caps, Graphite color.
- Rows: Inter Regular, 16px.

**Interaction Notes:**

- Rows have a hover effect (light gray background).
- "Sign Out" is Red.

**Vibe:**
System preferences. Clean and functional.
