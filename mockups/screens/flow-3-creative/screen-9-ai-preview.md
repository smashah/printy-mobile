## Flow 3: Creative Studio

### Screen 9: AI Preview

**Prompt:**

Design the AI Generation Result/Loading screen.

**Layout:**

- **Full Screen**: Focus on the image.
- **Loading State**: A "developing" animation.
- **Result State**: The final dithered image.

**Visual Centerpiece:**
The Image.

**Key Elements:**

- **Loading**:
  - "DREAMING..." text blinking.
  - A pixelated noise animation that slowly resolves into the image.
- **Result**:
  - The generated image in a 4x6 frame.
  - Action Bar: "Print", "Save", "Remix (Edit Prompt)".
  - "Feedback": Thumbs up/down.

**Interaction Notes:**

- Tapping the image zooms in.
- "Print" goes straight to the Printing modal (Screen 4 logic).

**Vibe:**
Digital Darkroom. Watching a Polaroid develop.
